About This Project

Objective:
To simulate a real-world credit risk portfolio and analyze Basel capital requirements.

What was built:

  • Multi-asset portfolio (Sovereign, Banks, Corporates)
  • IRB-based RWA calculations
  • Stress testing (Baseline, Adverse, Severe)
  • Interactive analytics dashboard

Tech Stack:
Python, JSON, JavaScript (ECharts)

Outcome:
End-to-end credit risk analytics platform with scenario-based insights.
