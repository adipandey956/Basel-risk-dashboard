About This Project

Objective:
To simulate a real-world credit risk portfolio and evaluate capital requirements under Basel frameworks.

What was built:

  • Multi-asset portfolio (Sovereign, Banks, Corporates)
  • IRB-based RWA calculations
  • Stress scenarios (Baseline, Adverse, Severe)
  • Interactive dashboard using ECharts

Tech Stack:
Python, JSON, JavaScript

Outcome:
End-to-end credit risk analytics platform demonstrating portfolio risk and capital sensitivity.
