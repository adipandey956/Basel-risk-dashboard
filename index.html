About This Project

Objective:
To simulate a real-world credit risk portfolio and analyze capital requirements under Basel frameworks.

What was built:
- Synthetic multi-asset credit portfolio (Sovereign, Banks, Corporates, Retail)
- IRB-based RWA and capital calculation
- Standardized Approach comparison across jurisdictions
- Stress testing (Baseline, Adverse, Severe scenarios)
- Interactive dashboard for risk visualization

Tech Stack:
Python, JSON, JavaScript (ECharts)

Credits:
Leveraged the open-source library creditriskengine and built a full analytics + visualization layer on top.

Outcome:
A complete end-to-end credit risk analytics platform demonstrating portfolio risk, capital sensitivity, and regulatory impact.
